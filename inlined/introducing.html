Introducing Bec G.

Your Australian based Turkey Travel Specialist

WHO ARE ALATURKA?

We are a leading travel agency that is based all throughout Turkey with our head office in Fethiye, located along the beautiful south coastline of Turkey. The 2 owners of Alaturka; Murat – Ned – Danisan and Rasit – Reggie – Kaya have been working in the Turkey travel industry since they were young and have over 40 years of experience between the two of them. I am Alaturka’s Turkey Travel Specialist and have lived in Turkey for the past 4 years, and have travelled it extensively for the past 20 years, but after having my first child 2 years ago, decided to come back to the place I call home – Sydney, Australia.

EXPERIENCE LIKE NO OTHER

At Alaturka we pride ourselves on knowing the best things to do in Turkey and the best way to do it. With inside local knowledge and the experienced traveller that has explored most corners of the country, we know what advice to give and how to make each of our guests fall in love with Turkey. Our tours are unique in that our guests are still the independent traveller, doing the local buses, eating at the local cafes, we just take the hassle and thinking out of the holiday and help our guests pre-plan and pre-book their holiday, so they can sit back, relax and see Turkey for all that is has to offer. Private group tours are also available.

WHAT CAN WE DO?

As we like to say – anything is possible in Turkey. Alaturka can book anything and everything for any guest on any budget. We can assist our guests with accommodation, day tours, transfers, cruises, transportation and general advice. But what we can also do is put together peoples full Turkey itineraries. Starting from arrival airport transfer, taking in the highlights of Turkey and seeing our guests off on their journey home. We also are the leading cruise company in Fethiye for all Sail Turkey holidays as shared cabin charter, specialising in famous Fethiye to Olympos route. We also have our own fleet of gulets/yachts to run private yacht charters on the southern coastline of Turkey. These cruises are one of our guests highlights of Turkey.
